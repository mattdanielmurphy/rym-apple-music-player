The sister list to Darkside Atmospherics, read that list's description if you'd like a little history. Long story short, these two lists exist to show what I see as a difference in sound between two styles of Atmospheric Drum and Bass. One style which was more ruff and hardcore influenced produced generally from 1993-1996, and another which we will be covering in this list, that is more Chill-Out and jazzy. This style drops a lot of the ruff break choppage and production styles for something thats more "truely" atmospheric, with cleaner mixed breaks, and more focus of melody and atmosphere.

Music itself may not have the power to directly enact social change; more so, it is an essential meditation on the now.
